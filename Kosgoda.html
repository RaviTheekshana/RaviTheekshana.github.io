About Kosgoda Turtle Hatchery

The Kosgoda Turtle Hatchery, located in Sri Lanka, is a renowned conservation project dedicated to protecting and nurturing sea turtles.
hatchery is essential to the survival of the endangered turtle species that inspired its establishment.
The extraordinary journey that turtle hatchlings take on their way to the sea can be seen by visitors to the hatchery, and they can also learn about the conservation efforts being made to safeguard these magnificent animals.
Additionally, the hatchery offers educational initiatives that promote the value of marine conservation.
Sea turtles require a safe haven, and the Kosgoda Turtle Hatchery plays a key role in preserving Sri Lanka's abundant marine biodiversity.

Conservation Efforts

A focal point for conservation efforts is Sri Lanka's Kosgoda Turtle Hatchery. Dedicated people and organizations put in endless effort to safeguard sea turtles that are in risk of extinction, encourage nesting, and guarantee the survival of hatchlings. They work to protect these wonderful creatures and their delicate environments for future generations via education, research, and community involvement.
